BBA (Honours)

Eligibility :- Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) in any stream. Course Fee :- Rs. 11500/- Per Semester. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a 4 years undergraduate Honours degree course which is divided into 8 semesters. BBA Course is designed to provide a basic understanding of management education and to train the students in communication skills effectively, which inculcate entrepreneurship skills and decision making.

BBA (Tourism & Hospitality)

Eligibility :- Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) in any stream. Course Fee :- Rs. 25000/- Per Semester. Bachelor of Business Administration in Hospitality & Tourism (BBA T&H) is a 4 years undergraduate Honours degree course which is divided into 8 semesters.The program has been designed to offer eligible candidates an advanced specialization in any of the various aspects of the hospitality industry, such as operations, front office, housekeeping, food and beverages, accounting, engineering/ maintenance, sales, public relations, security, etc.

Application Process

  1. Review Requirements: Check the eligibility criteria for your desired program.
  2. Prepare Documents: Gather all required documents (transcripts, certificates, etc.).
  3. Fill Online Application: Complete the application form accurately.
  4. Pay Application Fee: Submit the non-refundable application fee.
  5. Submit Application: Ensure all sections are complete before submission.
  6. Interview (if applicable): Attend the interview if shortlisted.
  7. Admission Decision: Await the admission decision notification.
